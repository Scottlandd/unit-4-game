Crystals Collector!!!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four gemstones below. By clicking on a gemstone you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, and you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it to add to your score.


Each time the game restarts, the values of each crystal will change.
